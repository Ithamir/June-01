Butternut Squash Soup

Ingredients

  1. Dry Ingredients
    • Carrot
    • Onion
    • Apple
  2. Wet Ingredients
    • Vegetable oil
    • Coconut milk
  3. Condiments
    • Garlic
    • Sage
    • Salt
    • Black pepper
    • Cayenne
    • Cinnamon
    • Nutmeg

Cooking Instructions

  1. Combine ingredients
    • Roughly dice dry ingredients
    • Mix with condiments and coconut milk
  2. Cooking
    • Slow cook for 3-4 hours
    • Remove sage
    • Add vegetable oil
  3. Blend
    • Blend until smooth consistency
  4. Finishing touches
    • Taste and reseason to satisfaction
  5. Serve and enjoy!
